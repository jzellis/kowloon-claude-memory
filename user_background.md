Josh is a professional writer and journalist, not just a developer. He wants Kowloon to read as elegantly as a print magazine. Readability, typography, and editorial aesthetics are first-class concerns — not afterthoughts.

**How to apply:** When making decisions about text rendering, truncation, whitespace, type hierarchy, or content display, think like a magazine editor, not just a programmer. "Does this read well?" is as important as "does this work correctly." Bring these instincts proactively — don't wait to be asked.
